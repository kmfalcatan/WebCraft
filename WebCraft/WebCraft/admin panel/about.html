Welcome to MedEquip Tracker, the advanced equipment management system tailored specifically for the College of Medicine of Westen Mindanao State University (WMSU). Developed by the skilled software engineering student team at Webcraft, our system aims to simplify and enhance the process of tracking and monitoring equipment within the college.

At MedEquip Tracker, we understand the critical importance of maintaining an accurate inventory and staying informed about the status of medical equipment. Our user-friendly platform offers a comprehensive solution for tracking equipment, enabling the College of Medicine to efficiently manage both new and older equipment based on their respective years.

With MedEquip Tracker, all equipment is conveniently categorized by year, ensuring easy organization and identification. Each equipment entry is accompanied by detailed information, empowering college staff to effortlessly access key data such as calibration requirements and current status.

Our system empowers College of Medicine employees by providing them with the information they need to make informed decisions regarding equipment usage and maintenance. Whether it's locating specific equipment, tracking its status, or scheduling calibration, MedEquip Tracker simplifies the process, saving valuable time and effort.

At Webcraft, we take pride in developing this system with the college's specific needs in mind. Our primary objective is to deliver a reliable and efficient solution that seamlessly manages equipment, ultimately contributing to the smooth functioning of the College of Medicine.
